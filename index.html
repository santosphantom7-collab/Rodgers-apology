For My Kezzie ❤️

An apology from the depths of my heart

My love Kezzie, I am deeply sorry for the pain I caused you. Hurting you is the greatest regret of my heart. Please believe me when I say — **you are the one I want.**

I promise to do better, to love you harder, to listen more, and to protect your heart. I never want to lose you, because life without you feels empty.

Please forgive me, my love. Let me prove to you that my heart belongs only to you.

A Poem For You 🌹

I’m sorry for the tears I made you cry,
For the lonely nights and every sigh.
I’m sorry for the pain I brought your way,
For the words I failed to properly say.

But know this truth, both deep and true,
My heart still beats in love with you.
Through every storm, through dark and light,
You are my peace, my soul’s delight.

If love could speak, it would say your name,
Again and again, always the same.
For in this world, so vast, so true,
There’s no one else — there’s only **you.**

I Love You Kezzie ❤️

I choose you today. I choose you tomorrow. I choose you forever.

Please forgive me. Let me love you the way you deserve.

Forever yours, Rodgers ❤️
